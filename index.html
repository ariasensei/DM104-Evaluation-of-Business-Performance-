Course Description

This course focuses on the analysis, measurement, and evaluation of business performance using information systems, key performance indicators (KPIs), and business analytics. It emphasizes techniques for assessing efficiency, effectiveness, and value creation in business processes, aligning performance evaluation with strategic goals, and using data to support managerial decision-making.

Learning Outcomes

By the end of the semester, you will be able to:

1

Explain the concepts and principles of business performance measurement, key performance indicators (KPIs), and analytics, and their alignment with organizational strategy.

2

Design, select, and apply KPIs and performance measurement frameworks to evaluate efficiency, effectiveness, and value creation in business processes.

3

Analyze and interpret performance data using descriptive and predictive analytics tools to support managerial decision-making.

4

Evaluate the effectiveness, limitations, and risks of performance measurement systems, and recommend improvements for better organizational outcomes.

5

Demonstrate ethical, responsible, and professional use of business performance data in accordance with legal, organizational, and social standards.
